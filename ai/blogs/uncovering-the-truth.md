## Buyer’s Agent vs Real Estate Agent: The Core Difference

When you walk into an open house, chances are the first person you meet is the real estate agent. But here’s the reality: no matter how friendly they are, they represent the seller’s interests, not yours.

### Real Estate Agent Responsibilities

A real estate agent is primarily hired by the seller. Their key responsibilities include:

- Marketing the property to attract buyers.
- Negotiating to secure the highest possible price for the seller.
- Advising the seller on pricing strategies.
- Managing offers, contracts, and inspections on behalf of the seller.

Their income generally comes from the real estate commission, which is a percentage of the final sale price. Naturally, this creates a strong incentive to achieve the best result for the vendor.

### Buyer’s Agent Responsibilities

A buyer’s agent, on the other hand, is engaged by the buyer. Their role is to protect and promote _your_ best interests by:

- Conducting in-depth research on properties and suburbs.
- Shortlisting properties that match your needs and budget.
- Negotiating with the selling agent to secure the lowest price possible.
- Providing unbiased advice on property value, risks, and opportunities.
- Bidding at auctions on your behalf with strategy and confidence.

At **Quantum Buyers Agents**, we pride ourselves on being your advocate throughout the process. Whether you’re a seasoned investor or a first home buyer, our mission is to give you clarity, confidence, and results.

## Why Real Estate Agents Represent Sellers, Not Buyers

It’s a common misconception that real estate agents “help” both buyers and sellers equally. In reality, they are contractually obligated to act in the seller’s best interests.

This means:

- The information they share is designed to benefit the seller.
- Their negotiation tactics aim to secure the _highest_ possible price.
- They may not highlight flaws or risks in the property.

This is where a buyer’s agent becomes invaluable. By having an independent advocate like **Quantum Buyers Agents** on your side, you can level the playing field and ensure your financial interests come first.
